Reading Passage

Hello! My name is Ana. I am from Mexico, but I live in Spain. I am Mexican, and my husband is Spanish. His name is Luis. He is tall and very kind. I am short and I have black hair. We have two children: a boy and a girl. Their names are Diego and Sofa.

Diego is 10 years old. He is smart and funny. Sofa is 7 years old. She is small and very friendly. We love our children very much.

We live in a small house near the city. The house has a living room, a kitchen, two bedrooms, and a bathroom. In the living room, we have a brown sofa, a small table, and a black TV. In the kitchen, there is a fridge, a table, and four chairs.

In our bedroom, Luis and I have a big bed, a lamp, and a closet. The children share a bedroom. They have two beds, a desk, and a bookshelf with many books.

Our house is not big, but it is clean and comfortable. I love my family and my home.
