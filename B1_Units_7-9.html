Reading Passage

When William was younger, he used to think that choosing a career was easy. He believed that studying business or economics would give him many opportunities. However, as he grew older, he realized that the process was more complex and full of challenges.

At university, William explored different fields such as engineering, computer science, and biology. He discovered that computer science was not as difficult as he expected, but it required a lot of practice. Engineering was as demanding as people said, and students have to manage many projects at the same time.

William also learned that students must organize their time well. They need to complete assignments, group projects, and daily chores while preparing for exams. These responsibilities were an important part of his education and personal growth.

During his studies, William worked part-time to gain experience. His job was not as exciting as his classes, but it helped him develop discipline. One of his biggest achievements was finishing a long project that took months of effort.

Now, William sees his career as a chance to grow and change. He knows that every challenge brings a new opportunity, and that learning is as important as success in any profession.

Reading Passage

For the last few months, Mark has been spending a lot of his free time watching different kinds of TV shows and movies. He has always enjoyed entertainment, but recently he has been paying more attention to how genres affect his mood. He usually watches animated movies and romantic comedies to relax, while thrillers and horror films are saved for weekends. Science fiction movies, on the other hand, make him think more about the future and technology.

Mark has also been following several TV shows. He has been watching a reality show about cooking competitions and enjoys game shows with his family.
