Reading Passage

Every Saturday, Marta visits her cousin Luis. Marta likes spending time with him because his house is always full of good food. When she arrives, Luis always prepares a small snack. This snack is usually a sandwich or some fruit. Marta’s favorite is this sandwich with cheese and tomatoes.

At lunchtime, they eat together in Luis’s kitchen. These meals are simple, but very tasty. They usually drink orange juice or water because those drinks are healthy. After eating, Ana and Luis sometimes share a chocolate cake that Luis buys at the bakery near his house. It is their favorite dessert.

After lunch, Marta and Luis often sit in the living room and talk about the people they know. Marta tells stories about her coworkers, and Luis talks about his classmates from university. They sometimes look at old photos and say things like, “These are our friends from school,” or “That is our teacher from English class.” Marta loves these conversations because they help her remember special moments.

Marta enjoys these Saturdays because she spends time with someone she loves, and she never leaves without saying: “This is my favorite day of the week!”

Reading Passage

Laura is a university student, and she also works part-time in a small office. Every day she has many things to do. In the morning, she makes her bed, has breakfast, and checks these study items in her backpack: her notebook, her laptop, and her pens. She always says, “This backpack is so heavy!”

At the office, Laura does different tasks. She usually answers emails and organizes documents. But today is different. Right now, she is doing a report for her boss, and she is using those papers on the desk to finish her work. Her coworker Tom is watching and says, “That looks difficult!”

In the afternoon, Laura goes to the library. She often studies there because it’s quiet. Today, she is studying for an exam, and she is making notes with these colorful markers. She feels tired, but she thinks, “I always work hard, and this helps me learn more.”

Even though her days are busy, Laura enjoys her routine. She believes these daily habits help her grow, and she is proud of the work she do
